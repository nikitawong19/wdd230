Target Audience

All business owners who want to grow their business, do business with someone who cares about their community, introduce their business to potential clients, or help the business community become stronger. There are no age, income, education, or experience restrictions. So long as their business is in Malaysia. Anyone with a mobile phone and a computer can access this website.

Scenarios

1. Steven owns a CCTV company and wishes to expand his operations. He wants to know the best way to introduce his company to potential clients through Chamber-sponsored events that attract other businesses and the general public.

2. Janet is the owner of a food franchise. She is experiencing difficulties in her business as a result of the post-effects of Covid-19. Her business problems are likely shared by her fellow men and women in the business community. What better way to talk through difficul or problem in her business than discussing with those who are walking on the same path as her.

3. Andrew is the owner of a local bike shop. He needs help in increasing his bike sales. He visited the website and saw the benefits of becoming a member of the chamber of commerce. He completes the form and submits it. He finally found a way to get help growing his business..

4. Jeff is a marketing director in his software company. He wants to increase sales for his company, so he goes to the website and contacts a company listed in the website's directory to provide consulting services to the company.

5. Daniel works for an event company as an event manager. He is looking for a way to connect with people in order to build a network and grow his business. He saw the chamber's events and decided to use them to promote his business.
