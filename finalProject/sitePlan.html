Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas.

Objective

The objective is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas. We want to promote healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience.

Target Audience

The target audience consists of adults with a middle to high income who are looking for healthy drinks. Customer range in age from 8 years old onwards. This drinks is suitable for all types of people, including singles, couples, families, groups, teenagers, adults and the elderly. They could be from different part of the country or tourists visiting the coastal area. Customers can use their phones or computers to access our website. They can contact us by calling us or leaving a message on our website.
