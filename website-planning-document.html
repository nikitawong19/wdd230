Site Purpose

Business professionals and thought leaders who do business in Malaysia or would like to do so are invited to join us to improve the quality of the business community by fostering regional economic prosperity. If you want to be a part of a Chamber that is at the heart of this growth, we invite you to join us today.

Objective

To form and create a business association for the interests and benefits of the business community and professionals. To unite and solidify the business community and professionals in the interest of the Malaysia economy. To guarantee and protect the interest of its Members in relation to any type of commercial laws or business regulations formulated or amended in Malaysia. To represent and provide assistance to its Members in trade discussions, purchasing, sales, marketing and others whether in or outside Malaysia.
